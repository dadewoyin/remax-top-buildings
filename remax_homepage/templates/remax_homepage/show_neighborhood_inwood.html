One of the many fast-growing uptown New York City neighborhoods, Inwood gets its unique feel from its sense of community and integrity to culture. Inwood is located at the very top of New York City, and is bordered by Washington Heights.

Many people may discount Inwood for its distance from the main events of New York City, when in fact Inwood itself is a location-a beautiful haven with a small-town feel inside the city. The residents who live here absolutely love Inwood Hill Park, as it has endless facilities and athletic fields for everyone to enjoy. You can truly view some beautiful nature here, which feels like an escape from the rest of New York City.

If you are worried about the transportation, it’s not as bad as you think. Inwood is very close to LaGuardia for airplane travel, and as for the city, the subway takes only 30 minutes to get to Times Square from Inwood. Many people are flocking to this area because they are realizing how great the location is within itself-with authentic restaurants and nightlife, there’s no need to even travel downtown.

In terms of real estate, Inwood has grown in popularity tremendously over the last couple of years. The sale and rental prices, however, are still way below the median price for New York City, making this an affordable option for you to call home. With a hot real estate market, this neighborhood could also be a good investment for the future. Come check out some properties in this beautiful area today!

Beans-and-Vines

The highly cultural aspects of this neighborhood make the food of Inwood even more delicious, as it is all authentic and homemade.

One of the best restaurants in the area is Beans and Vines, a tiny restaurant that is known for its Yuca fries and Gourmet Chimi. With delicious wine and coffee, this place serves up some of the best drinks, but the food may be the best part. The international feel to the restaurant only adds to its mystique and elegance, making it the perfect opportunity for a night out.

Another great choice is Indian Road Cafe, a popular American restaurant that serves up amazing food and drinks to boot. You can try everything from a banh mi sandwich to lobster mac and cheese at this electric joint, not to mention their delicious coffee and wine.

If instead you’re looking for a juicy steak, Guadalupe will definitely not disappoint. They are known for their currasco, sangria, and guacamole, as well as their other drinks which are sure to leave a buzz. Make sure that you try out this delectable treat before leaving Inwood!


For some of the best Chinese food of Inwood, try out Amy’s Restaurant. Known for their fresh noodles and exceptional delivery services, Amy’s will definitely provide you with a great quality of food for the price. Their menu is also healthier than most, ensuring that you will get a great meal that will sit well with you.

Another amazing Asian restaurant is Yummy Thai. Check out their eccentric ambience, fantastic drinks, and food to please at this Inwood restaurant. They are known for their spring rolls and pad thai, but you are free to venture out and try one of their many different options.

If you’re looking for a great breakfast stop or just something quick to eat, check out G’s Coffee Shop. All the residents here love the Mexican breakfast burrito, as well as quick tacos and convenience store. The hole in the wall will certainly impress and exceed all of your expectations.

Inwood Local

Even though the neighborhood is relatively quiet, the nightlife here in Inwood is always hopping.

First, check out Inwood Local, a fabulous bar with an extensive wine selection in addition to bar food to enjoy. They also have 16 beers on tap, 14 of those local selections, which really adds to the appeal of the bar. This bar can get really loud during busy hours, so if you’d like to have an energetic evening, Inwood Local is for you.

Another bar option is Corcho Wine Room, which offers some impeccable wine choices that match the caliber of live music that plays in the venue. Their bartender makes some terrific craft cocktails, and is very knowledgeable in wine selection, to select a perfect pairing for your dinner or just a casual drink. The cozy environment is a great place to catch up and savor a night with friends.


The club nightlife in the Inwood area is also active and colorful, leaving you with a great night to dance and party away.

First, check out El Tina Bar, a Spanish dance club with drinks that are almost as good as the dancing! You will certainly lose track of time while enjoying your night here.

Or, try out Nitro if you’re really looking for a cool party and you’re ready to dance. Their small space makes dancing crowded and intense, so this is surely the main event of the night. Nitro has a really cool all-white venue with dry ice machines and other lighting techniques to make this night special.

Negro Claro

Another hip lounge is Negro Claro, located in the heart of Inwood with all of the tools necessary for the perfect party. Drawing a large young crowd, this club manages to keep everything upscale and orderly for the environment. You will definitely not be disappointed by the party that Negro Claro puts on through its great atmosphere.

Inwood Gourmet

This store is definitely a resident favorite, so follow suit and shop here today.

Inwood Hill Park

The main park located in Inwood is very large, named Inwood Hill Park. They offer everything from barbecue areas to tennis courts, basketball courts to roller hockey, as well as baseball field, fitness equipment, dog-friendly areas, soccer fields, hiking trails, nature centers, and so much more.

For runners and walkers, there is always a new trail and different path that you can check out for a diversified routine. An athlete will definitely enjoy the top-of-the-line courts and equipment available in the park, including fitness equipment, baseball field, handball fields, basketball courts, roller hockey, and soccer fields. There is surely a great opportunity for you to please every one of your family/friends if you take a trip to Inwood Hill Park.

Or, those looking for something less active can take a look at the beautiful wildlife dotting the landscape of Inwood Park; maybe you even want to have a picnic in the amazing area. Your children can play at any of the three playgrounds at the expansive park while you relax and take in the scenery.

Therefore, there are so many options for you to choose from for a wonderful day at Inwood Hill Park. Stop by today if you’re interested in anything from a day full of excitement to relaxation.

Kips Bay is a trapezoid-shaped area that is bordered by Murray Hill, Gramercy Park, and Midtown South.

Known for its serenity and high level of medical care, Kips Bay is often overlooked when discussing New York City neighborhoods-but it shouldn’t be. The neighborhood is actually one of the biggest steals you can find within NYC, as the inexpensive property prices, friendly environment, and impeccable location make this an ideal place to call home.

One thing you definitely won’t have to worry about in Kips Bay is proximity to hospitals. With NYU Langone Medical Center, Bellevue Hospital Center, and the Manhattan VA Hospital all located within the neighborhood, as well as the NYU College of Dentistry and School of Medicine in the area, there are no shortages of doctors to address all of your health needs. And, the medical colleges in the area help bring a vibrancy to the neighborhood, without being too overt.

Another perk of Kips Bay is the Kips Bay AMC, which is favored by residents in the neighborhood. This Kips Bay theatre provides a wonderful source of entertainment without having to veer out of the neighborhood, while still maintaining a quiet atmosphere.

Because of its low activity rates, Kips Bay also has a lower crime rate than the Manhattan average. In addition to the low crime, the low rents are very desirable for young professionals and families in the area, who make up the predominance of the Kips Bay residents. With a median age of 31, Kips Bay has a distinctly young feel, while still maintaining the maturity of a quiet neighborhood.

Therefore, whether you’re excited for the quiet yet central neighborhood, superb hospitals, or even the Kips Bay AMC, Kips Bay will exceed your expectations for a residential neighborhood within New York City.

Marchi's Restaurant

There are many fine dining options to choose from within the small neighborhood of Kips Bay. Located at 31st Street and 2nd Avenue, Marchi’s Restaurant offers old school yet authentic Italian cuisine. It is a family owned and operated legendary restaurant that has been serving New Yorkers since 1930. Once here, you will be well taken care of by attentive servers. The outdoor garden dining area feels amazing when you can enjoy both your favorite dish and the breeze of a beautiful day.

If you’d like to try a new Japanese restaurant, visit O Ya. After it just opened this summer, O Ya has already made it to the list of Best Restaurants Ranked in Yelp with their freshest sashimi and dedication to perfection. They must be doing something right, so experience it for yourself.

Vatan Indian Vegetarian is definitely a gem in Kips Bay for both vegetarians and non-vegetarian customers. The Gujarati Thali entrees and Indian style interior decorations might convince you that you are actually in India, or at least enhance your meal.

At the American restaurant The Cannibal, you can try some unique specialty dishes that are sure to please. While some might consider slow roasted pig head a little intimidating, their exquisite layers of flavors make this dish their specialty.


If you’re looking for some great food selections in Midtown, Kips Bay’s takeout does not disappoint.

For pasta lovers with big appetites, Bistango is highly recommended. This restaurant provides appetizing Italian cuisine with a gluten free option to suit any dietary need. Spinach ravioli with pesto sauce is their specialty, so make sure to try it!

Or, check out Oishi Bay if you like the sound of a Japanese restaurant/sushi bar that offers not only traditional sushi rolls and sashimi but also creative original sushi rolls. This exceptional find comes at an affordable price, making it a fantastic option to enjoy for lunch.

For something exotic, Sahara’s Turkish Cuisine provides a variety of Middle Eastern, Mediterranean, and Turkish cuisine from 11am to 11pm. The falafel here is so authentic that it raises the bar for all other Middle Eastern food in the area.

If you’re looking for something fast and easy, Patrizia’s Pizza and Pasta will have your take-out orders ready quickly and has a discount of 15% off for NYU students and faculty. Clams, vegetables, cheese plate, calamari, panini, pasta, and pizzas here are both delicious and modestly priced.

New York Comedy Club

Kips Bay houses the longest-running production house in continuous operation in New York City called Liberty Studios. Founded in 1963, it now produces films, videos, advertising, and e-commerce productions. Liberty Studios has received both Emmy and Academy Award nominations.

New York Comedy Club features different comedians from time to time and sometimes during the weekend two shows are hosted continuously by different comedians. Not only does this comedy club feature hilarious comedians, but also an intimate atmosphere that makes comedian-audience interaction possible. Last but not least, soft drinks, alcoholic drinks, and finger-licking snacks are available for your enjoyment.

For a live stage performance, Richmond Shepard Theatre is another favorable choice. The theatre usually performs plays, but once in a while live music performances are also held here. It is one of the top-notch theaters in the city due to its small setting that provides intimacy between performers and audience.


Kips Bay also features many art galleries to explore on a rainy day or in your free time.

For a technologically advanced viewing experience, try out Holographic Studios, located on East 26th Street between 2nd Avenue and 3rd Avenue. The gallery is famous for its hologram displays throughout the premises. Established in 1979, it is the world’s oldest gallery of holography and it sells amazing holograms, which make cool and amusing gifts to admire.

If you are interested in watching how paintings are beautifully framed, E Mayan Studio is highly recommended. Paintings from 19th and 20th century to contemporary scenes are framed here. Besides a great selection of paintings, sketches and photographic work are also on display in the art gallery, leaving you with a variety of options to view.

Therefore, Kips Bay offers many art galleries to choose from for amazing exhibitions of different varieties.

Kips Bay has many hospitals in the area should the need arise, with a top quality of service and hospital.

For a top rated hospital right in Kips Bay, visit NYU Langone Medical Center. The medical center has numerous specialties ranging from airway, breathing, and lung conditions to kidney and liver conditions. As one of the nation’s premier academic medical centers, Langone scored first for overall patient safety and quality among leading academic medical centers.

Bellevue Hospital Center is the oldest continuously operating hospital in America. They trace their roots back to 1736 when a six-bed infirmary opened on the second floor of the New York City Almshouse. Often referred to as a national treasure, Bellevue defines the very best traditions of public medicine as a public service vital to the wellbeing of our civil society.

NYU Langone Hospital

Another hospital that lies on the East side of 1st Avenue is the Manhattan VA Hospital. It has services in acute medicine, surgery, acute psychiatry, neurology, and rehabilitation medicine. As for other types of conditions, outpatient service is available.


Vincent F. Albano, Jr. Playground

At the northwestern corner of 29th Street and 2nd Avenue, Vincent F. Albano, Jr. Playground is a fenced playground open during the daytime with a large playground set for children and benches & tables for adults. Its welcoming and intimate environment is perfect for a play-date among kids, or for you to relax and enjoy a beautiful day.

Perhaps you’d like to visit Bellevue South Park, which is located along the west side of Mt. Carmel Place. This park is a beloved recreational destination for both children and adults, especially with volleyball and basketball courts available during the day time. A sculpture named Skaggerak displayed in Bellevue South Park is a nice addition to the community and the surrounding area.

Located at North Side of 23rd Street is Asser Levy Recreation Center, including Asser Levy Playground, Asser Levy Pool, and Asser Levy Recreation Center. Visitors can play ping pong, badminton, chess, soccer, football, tee-ball, as well as exercise, jog, practice yoga or simply sit in the shaded bench to unwind. There is an indoor pool reserved for members only, but the 120-foot wide outdoor pool is open to the public for free during the summer, making this a children’s hotspot during the warmer months.

Murray Hill is a rectangular area in Midtown East, bordered by Turtle Bay, Kips Bay, and Midtown.

This neighborhood is usually considered one of the most desirable ones in vicinity of Midtown business district due to its location and affordability. Murray Hill consists of a splendid mix of brownstone apartments, condominiums, coops, and row houses, which are classic characteristics of the Murray Hill Historic District. Murray Hill now is moving away from its traditionally quiet reputation towards hip and more fashionable, which is drawing in a younger crowd to the area.

Known as much for its nightlife as its architecture and location, Murray Hill is a spot that you want to spend the night at. Whether a lively club or a relaxed bar, there is definitely a scene for everybody, even within the small area. Residents flock from all over New York City to experience this classic nightlife scene.

While in the area, many people love to try the various restaurants, and with the diverse offerings from steak to Japanese, you will certainly enjoy the wide palate that Murray Hill provides. For any health considerations, the Murray Hill Medical Group will take care of your needs. Known for their great service and wide capabilities, Murray Hill Medical Group is a favorite for residents and visitors alike for their health concerns.

In terms of real estate, Murray Hill offers considerably lower prices than other neighborhoods in the area. This makes the Murray Hill area a perfect place to consider for your next rental or purchase, as you will certainly get an exceptional bang for your buck. With an increased value based on units sold, as well as a dramatic increase of listings, it is the time to move to Murray Hill.

If you’re looking for a great place to eat in Murray Hill, their fine dining will not disappoint!

Or, check out La Brochette, a Kosher steakhouse with a great selection of sushi and sashimi. Not only is the food here irresistible, but also the ambiance is perfect for a romantic evening. With candles, delicious food, lovely decorations, and thoughtful service, taking your date here for an amorous dinner can never go wrong.

For a traditional American cuisine with personal chef service, Juni on 31st Street between Madison Avenue and 5th Avenue is your best choice. With exquisite layouts of all the dishes, dining here not only pleases the palate but excites your eyes. For dinner, you can simply choose from herbivore, omnivore, and chef’s tasting menus, and then your personal chef will carefully select ingredients accordingly to prepare a series of dishes for you to enjoy.

Pierpont Morgan Library

If you’re looking to relax and enjoy some of the great attractions in the area, look no further than Murray Hill itself.

The most popular attraction of the neighborhood is The Morgan Library & Museum, a historical site that houses some of the most beautiful relics of history. If you’re interested in viewing anything from famous manuscripts to books, music to drawings, or even ancient works of art, visit The Morgan today. Although admission is reasonably priced, if you come on Free Friday, your admission is free!

Another cultural hub located in Murray Hill is Scandinavia House, a collection of works to represent the Swedish, Finnish, Danish, Icelandic, and Norwegian cultures. They offer movies from these countries, as well as art exhibits which are representative of the culture of Scandinavia. This collection is truly fascinating and informative, so be sure to try it out today!

Check out one of these culture centers today to step back and admire great pieces of history.

With a fine selection of artwork that you can take a look at while waiting for your framing job to be completed, this is the perfect combination of art and service.

St. Vartan Park

Even though the neighborhood is small, Murray Hill also provides some great green areas for children to play and adults to relax at.

First, check out St. Vartan Park, a facility that offers amenities ranging from baseball fields and basketball courts to football fields and playgrounds. There is also a lot of green space to enjoy a picnic or a stroll through the fresh air, which is a perfect place to collect your thoughts or just unwind. The park was recently renovated in 2000, and now features new playground equipment, benches, curbs, and fences, which only adds to the appeal of the beautiful space. Be sure to check out this location today!

Another park located in the Murray Hill area is Murray Hill Playground, which is much smaller yet exciting for kids! There are handball courts on the premises, as well as an open grassy area to lounge around on. You should definitely try out this gem if you are looking for a treat!

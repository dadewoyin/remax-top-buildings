Carnegie Hill is a sophisticated neighborhood located on the Upper East Side, which is bordered by East Harlem, Yorkville, and Upper East Side.

Known for its historic buildings and residential appeal, Carnegie Hill is currently the most expensive northern neighborhood of New York City. While there are no longer functioning mansions in the area, the existing mansions have been converted into office buildings and apartments within the space. Even with a demand for more real estate, many legal blockades have been placed on upcoming projects to preserve the character of the area. Therefore, Carnegie Hill has been able to maintain a lot of its historic appeal while ushering in a new generation.

Because of its ideal location on the Upper East Side, Carnegie Hill offers some of the best restaurants in all of New York City, particularly for fine dining. If you’d like to try anything from contemporary American to sushi, the cuisine here will not disappoint a sophisticated palate. Or, if shopping is more your interest, then make sure to stop by Chanel or Urban Outfitters located in the area. One of the perks of living in Carnegie Hill is the proximity to incredible attractions, especially the Metropolitan Museum of Art. You can always find something interesting to do here, such as visiting the Jewish Museum NYC.

Many people flock to Carnegie Hill due to the amazing architecture, great school systems (Hunter College High School is located in the area), and proximity to both Midtown and Uptown. As such, real estate prices are higher than the New York City average, and the desirability is only continuing to increase. Be sure to consider a rental or purchase in Carnegie Hill today!

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

In Carnegie Hill, you are definitely going to want to check out one of the many museums.

One of these is none other than The Metropolitan Museum of Art, one of the best museums around the world. You will be amazed by the ancient to modern-age work of different countries. Walking through this museum is like walking through time, so hop in that time machine as soon as you can.

Next up, we have the amazing Guggenheim Museum, where you can admire the brilliant architecture. It is a place to make a whole experience out of and find something new every time you come.

The Frick Collection is internationally recognized as a premier museum and research center. It is known for its distinguished Old Master paintings and outstanding examples of European sculpture and decorative arts. From Rembrandt to Vermeer, Bellini to Goya, and Gainsborough to Whistler, you will certainly enjoy the amazing artwork that will blow you away

The brilliant artwork located within the Carnegie Hill area has to be seen to be believed; once you do, you will wish you came earlier!


Carnegie Hill also offers some beautiful displays of art for your viewing pleasure.

Gagosian Gallery is one of the best in the area for modern art, producing exhibitions by many legendary artists of the modern art era. Come check it out to view some stunning, timeless artwork.

Or, another art gallery of choice is Erarta New York, which showcases contemporary art of the New York City area, especially that of Russian artists. With an emphasis on Russian art internationally, Erata seeks to spread the beauty of Russian artwork everywhere.

Hunter College High School

Carnegie Hill houses perhaps the best public high school of New York City, as well as several other elementary schools that are just as good.

Students flock from all over New York City to attend Hunter College High School, a public school with some of the best college placement rates of the entire district. Students who are able to pass the exams and gain a spot in this prestigious high school often attend the top-ranked universities after this education, ensuring that the level of academics is very high.

There are also two independent day schools in the Carnegie Hill area, namely Brearley School and Chapin School. The Brearley School is distinguished in its collaborative and community efforts, which they use in order to enrich the lives of the girls at their school. Chapin School is also all-girls, and instead seeks to focus on the intellectual and personal growth of each girl within their system. Both are great options for girls looking for an independently successful organization.


The only university located in Carnegie Hill is New York School of Interior Design. This four year university offers undergraduate and graduate programs, which are ranked #3 and #1 in the nation, respectively. Evidently, this is the spot to come to if you are interested in interior design, due to the credentials and academic caliber of the university.

The Fhitting Room

Gyms in Carnegie Hill are everywhere, so you will have no problem finding one that suits your workout style.

Many people in Carnegie Hill enjoy EVF Performance, which is a half Cross Fit, half normal workout facility. In addition to Cross Fit, they have conditioning, strength, and rowing classes for every athletic and fitness level to try out. The coaches there are very professional and friendly, so you will certainly appreciate the atmosphere here.

Or, for something even more intense, try out The Fhitting Room, a High Intensity Training fitness studio. They pride themselves on their small class sizes and intimate workout environments, giving you the best chance at fitness success possible. Their high-energy workouts will certainly deliver the body-changing experience that you’re looking for.

So, whatever the gym you choose, you will definitely get the exercise you need while staying in Carnegie Hill.

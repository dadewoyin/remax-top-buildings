A perfect balance between wealth and practicality, the Upper West Side is one of the favorite residential neighborhoods of New York City. Upper West Side is bordered by Lincoln Square, Morningside Heights, and Central Park South.

One of the many things that the Upper West Side is known for is the infamous Upper West Side restaurants. The fine dining selections in this neighborhood are simply superb, with chefs from Jean Georges to John Fraser taking residence in this area. Even though Upper West Side restaurants are often very expensive, there are also some surprisingly affordable cheap eats if you are willing to look for hole-in-the-wall locations. Regardless of where you choose to dine, the Upper West side will definitely have the perfect choice for you.

Another attractive quality of the Upper West Side is the attractions that the neighborhood provides, from the Hayden Planetarium to Metropolitan Opera. These high class venues will certainly astound you with their elegance, and the attractions that follow are also delightful.

In terms of real estate opportunities, even though the Upper West Side is known for being a very expensive neighborhood, it is actually more affordable than people can imagine. With sale prices and rental averages only slightly above the New York City average, this is a great place to reside in if you’re looking for a well-maintained neighborhood that is perfect for families and young residents alike. As one of the most desirable neighborhoods, consider buying/renting from the Upper West Side today.

Dovetail

Matching the high pricepoint of the Upper West Side, there are an abundance of high-quality restaurants to choose from on a night out.

If you want a meal that will absolutely blow you away, no Upper West Side restaurant can beat the elegance of Jean-Georges. A sampling of French, American, and Asian influences, Jean-Georges food will certainly impress even the harshest of critics. Their pre-fixe menu or tasting menu is the best way to get a real sense of the menu, or you could try an item a la carte from their constantly updated menu.

Another very luxurious option is Dovetail, which showcases John Fraser’s masterpieces in an intimate and upscale setting. With a variety of pre-fixe choices that cycle throughout the week, you can definitely taste your fair share of this contemporary American cuisine.

For an amazing Mediterranean restaurant, Boulud Sun is the best of the best. With a modern layout and fresh colors throughout the space, the restaurant feels as fresh as the food. Whether you’re in the mood for a filling pasta or flavorful fish, Boulud Sun will certainly provide the taste that you’re looking for.


If you’re looking for a terrific delivery, takeout, and restaurant stop, Grill 212 is the perfect stop for you. Their wide variety of Middle Eastern foods offer a delectable choice for every customer, including generous portions and personable service. They also offer a complimentary salad bar to accompany many of the dishes, which is a tremendous addition to an already complete meal.

Or, try out Anthi’s Greek Food, a take-out only shop that offers great quality at a low cost-a typical hole in the wall location. With authenticity in check, you can try some of the original favorite Greek food available-just watch out for the relatively small portion sizes! Come give this small business with a big bite a try today.

In terms of authenticity, The Great Burrito trumps all others in its fresh Mexican food offerings. Another hole in the wall, be careful during busy hours as the location can get crowded, but the food at the end is definitely worth the hassle. All dishes accurately portray their Mexican roots, in a way that could not possibly be more delicious.

A classic grocery store choice is Trader Joe’s, where you’ll be met by a reliable standard of excellence in the products that you purchase. The fresh quality of their premade food, even when frozen, will surely amaze and delight you. To top it off, their reasonable prices will definitely keep you coming back again and again.

Another impeccable choice for groceries is West 82nd Grocery, a smaller store with an authentic vibe and cheap prices. The service here is personable and friendly, so you certainly have a great time shopping here.

Hayden Planetarium

You can choose from making stuffed animals, or painting pottery, mosaics, or ceramics, for there is definitely an art style for every child. For a more musical attraction, The Metropolitan Opera is a great choice. With everyone dressed in their best attire, the entire venue exudes opulence and elegance, which leaves you speechless by the time you step through the door. Riverside Park

Of course, Central Park is the main greenery spot for most of the residents of the Upper West Side, But, if you’re interested in trying out a new location and different environment, there are other parks as well that you can choose from to enjoy.

First, Riverside Park is a very long and thin park lining the West Side of the Upper West Side, and ventures almost all the way up Upper Manhattan. With a beautiful landscape and epic views sandwiching in a functional bike/walking trail, this park is perfect for somebody active that is looking for an escape and beautiful location to cherish. Riverside Park also features other amenities, such as baseball fields, basketball courts, eateries, fitness equipment, marinas, playgrounds, skate parks, tennis courts, and much more.

Even though small, Theodore Roosevelt Park is rated one of the best small parks of New York City. Featuring a dog run and green space, residents love to come here and enjoy a private and serene location that is even less populated than Central Park. Come check out their beautiful spaces today!

The Flatiron District is a downtown neighborhood located near Gramercy Park and Chelsea, and named after the iconic Flatiron Building on 23rd Street.

Because of its close proximity to the main attractions of New York City, as well as its professional appeal, the Flatiron District maintains a fusion of a working class and tourist environment. This neighborhood is lively and constantly offers new happenings, so you will always be busy if you end up in Flatiron.

One of the main attractions of the Flatiron District is obviously the Flatiron Building, which was completed in 1902 and is now a National Historic Landmark. Countless tourists and residents alike flock to the Flatiron Building every day to enjoy its beauty and unusual shape. Right next to the Flatiron Building lies Madison Square Park, a beloved beautiful 6.2-acre public park. Madison Square Park has been serving New Yorkers with numerous art shows, music events, dance performances, and children’s activities throughout the years, and is a favorite for its annual street vendor gatherings.

Flatiron is one of the most sought after areas Downtown, and as such, real estate prices are significantly higher than the New York City average. With a lower turnaround rate, you’ll be lucky to snag one of these amazingly located properties.

Therefore, the Flatiron District is the perfect spot to experience the vibrancy of New York City while still maintaining a professional environment.

Therefore, Flatiron will definitely provide exactly the type of entertainment that you are looking for.

Laughing Lotus

Unsurprisingly due to its amazing location, the Flatiron District has recently been nicknamed the Fitness District. The workout centers definitely live up to the new title, with these amenities being a key factor for residents of this area.

If you’re looking for a vibrant new workout opportunity, visit the 305 Fitness Center. This gym features a workout dance party style with a young, enthusiastic environment to accompany it.

Or, if you are looking for the perfect yoga spot, try Laughing Lotus. This yoga center features a unique combination of high energy yoga, and provides the ideal atmosphere for exercise.

For those looking for a country club-esque workout facility, the New York Health and Racquet Club is renowned for its amenities. They offer a pool, sun deck, steam room, and sauna along with their large workout facilities and classes.


In case of an injury or sickness, the health quality of the Flatiron District is superb. You may want to check into the Bellevue Hospital Center. Distinguished as the oldest hospital that is still in operation, over 500,000 patients make rounds through Bellevue every year. Besides its great all-around service and care, Bellevue hosts the only Psychiatric Emergency Service for children in all of New York. In fact, patients travel from all over the city to this hospital, ensuring its quality and utility to Flatiron. There are also many options of distinction at any of the surrounding specialty medical centers that are at your disposal in the case of a rare circumstance. Whatever the ailment, Flatiron is sure to provide premier health care to its residents.

Madison Square Park

Flatiron is located right next to Madison Square Park, where children and adults alike can savor their food options, greenery, and playground. Founded in 1847, this park is renowned for hosting many events and activities throughout the years alongside its iconic three statues. Madison Square Park features up-and-coming artwork for everyone to enjoy, with frequent exhibits as well as sculptures located throughout the park. There are also constant events, including concerts and playground exhibits, for everyone to love. Or, come to the park to relax from the hustle and bustle of the Flatiron neighborhood and to have a good time. Regardless of your activity of choice, you will be sure to enjoy Flatiron’s beautiful Madison Square Park.
